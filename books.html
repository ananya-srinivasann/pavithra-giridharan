Gallivanting With Ganesha

​Neel and Maya discover the magical powers of a special puzzle box that was gifted to Neel. They meet Ganesha, a beloved Hindu God, who takes them on exciting adventures. Join them as they explore stories from Hinduism, learn valuable lessons from Hindu Gods and in the process earn puzzle pieces in order to solve the final puzzle.​

Author's Note

Stories from Hinduism have always had a special place in my heart from when I was a child. This book emerged from my desire to see Hinduism represented more in children's literature in America.

As my children were growing up, I observed that there were limited options for chapter books that connected them with their Hindu heritage. My hope is that this book provides today’s young reader an opportunity to explore the rich world of Hinduism from a context that resonates with their experiences.

​My family has been a significant source of inspiration and encouragement for me throughout this journey. Though my children have outgrown this genre, I hope this serves as a meaningful resource for young readers and help them connect with their Hindu culture and heritage.
